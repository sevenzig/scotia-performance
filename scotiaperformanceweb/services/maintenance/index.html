Vehicle Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential to keep your vehicle running at its best and to prevent costly repairs down the road. At Scotia Performance, our certified technicians provide comprehensive maintenance services to extend the life of your vehicle and ensure optimal performance.

Oil Change Services

Regular oil changes are vital to maintaining your engine's health and performance. We offer various oil change options to meet your vehicle's specific needs:

  • Conventional oil changes
  • Synthetic blend oil changes
  • Full synthetic oil changes
  • High-mileage oil formulations
  • Diesel engine oil changes

Every oil change includes a comprehensive multi-point inspection to identify any potential issues before they become major problems.

Brake Maintenance

Your vehicle's braking system is essential for safety. Our brake maintenance services help ensure your brakes are functioning properly:

  • Brake pad/shoe inspection and replacement
  • Rotor/drum inspection, resurfacing, and replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and replacement
  • Caliper and wheel cylinder service
  • Brake line inspection
  • ABS system diagnosis and repair

Regular brake maintenance can prevent costly repairs and ensure your vehicle stops safely when you need it to.

Scheduled Maintenance

Following your vehicle manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule is the best way to protect your investment and ensure reliability:

  • 30,000 mile services
  • 60,000 mile services
  • 90,000 mile services
  • Timing belt replacement
  • Fluid checks and replacements
  • Filter replacements (air, fuel, cabin)
  • Spark plug replacement
  • Serpentine belt inspection and replacement

We follow factory-recommended service intervals specific to your vehicle's make and model to maintain warranty coverage and optimal performance.

State Inspection

As a New York State certified inspection station, we provide thorough vehicle inspections to ensure your vehicle meets all safety and emissions requirements:

  • Safety inspections
  • Emissions testing
  • Brake system inspection
  • Steering and suspension inspection
  • Light and signal inspection
  • Tire and wheel inspection

Our certified technicians will perform your inspection efficiently, and if any issues are found, we can make the necessary repairs to help you pass inspection.

Wheel Alignment

Proper wheel alignment is crucial for even tire wear, optimal fuel efficiency, and safe handling. Our alignment services include:

  • Two-wheel alignment
  • Four-wheel alignment
  • Camber adjustment
  • Caster adjustment
  • Toe adjustment
  • Suspension inspection

Signs you may need an alignment include uneven tire wear, vehicle pulling to one side, steering wheel not centered when driving straight, or feeling vibrations while driving.
