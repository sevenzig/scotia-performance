Performance & Tuning Services

At Scotia Performance, we specialize in taking your vehicle to the next level. Our expert technicians are passionate about performance and have the training and equipment to maximize your vehicle's potential. From simple bolt-on modifications to complete engine builds, we can help you achieve your performance goals.

ECU Tuning

Our ECU tuning services use advanced software and diagnostic equipment to reprogram your vehicle's Engine Control Unit (ECU). This customized approach optimizes fuel delivery, ignition timing, and boost pressure for improved horsepower, torque, and throttle response.

What We Offer:

  • Custom ECU mapping
  • Dyno tuning
  • Performance chip installation
  • Fuel delivery optimization
  • Boost control adjustment

Our tuning service is tailored to your specific vehicle and performance goals, whether you're looking for more power, improved fuel economy, or enhanced drivability.

Performance Tuning

Performance tuning goes beyond ECU modifications to include mechanical upgrades that enhance your vehicle's capabilities. Our comprehensive approach evaluates your entire drivetrain to identify the most effective upgrades for your performance goals.

Performance Services Include:

  • Cold air intake installation
  • Performance camshaft upgrades
  • Throttle body modifications
  • Fuel system upgrades
  • Transmission tuning
  • Suspension optimization

We'll work with you to create a customized performance package that balances power, reliability, and budget considerations.

Exhaust Systems

A performance exhaust system improves engine breathing, increases horsepower, and enhances your vehicle's sound. Our exhaust services range from simple muffler replacements to complete custom fabrication.

Exhaust Services:

  • Cat-back exhaust systems
  • Headers and manifolds
  • High-flow catalytic converters
  • Custom exhaust fabrication
  • Performance mufflers
  • Exhaust tuning for optimal sound

We carry premium brands and can design a system that matches your performance requirements while achieving your desired sound profile.

Forced Induction

Forced induction systems dramatically increase engine power by compressing air before it enters the combustion chamber. Whether you're interested in superchargers, turbochargers, or upgrading your existing system, our team has the expertise to help.

Forced Induction Services:

  • Turbocharger installation and upgrades
  • Supercharger systems
  • Intercooler installation
  • Boost controllers
  • Supporting modifications (fuel system, cooling, etc.)
  • Custom fabrication for unique applications

We carefully evaluate your engine's components to ensure reliability while achieving significant power gains through forced induction.

Engine Building

From rebuilding your existing engine to creating a custom high-performance powerplant, our engine building services are comprehensive. We use premium components and precise machining to build engines that deliver reliability and performance.

Engine Building Services:

  • Complete engine rebuilds
  • Performance engine builds
  • Cylinder head porting and polishing
  • Stroker kits
  • Forged internal components
  • Precision machining
  • Balanced rotating assemblies

Every engine we build is thoroughly tested and tuned to ensure it meets our exacting standards before being returned to your vehicle.
